## Challenges in design
- Design Characteristics
	- Links societal needs with technological solutions
	- Teamwork
	- Constraints on time and resources

- Key Challenge: **Fail Fast**
  Eliminate unfeasible alternatives as soon as possible without rejecting them for unjustified reasons


## SGPDP (Stage-Gate-Product-Development-Process)
1. Creating the project charter, e.g. Innovation maps 
   *(customer value proposition)*
2. Concept 
   *(consumer needs -> technical specifications)*
3. Feasibility
4. Manufacturing
5. Product introduction
